The Problem

It Starts With A Farmer

Before any meal reaches your plate, it begins with a farmer. A farm. A seed in the soil.
From planting to harvest, that journey is long — and increasingly uncertain.

Imagine the Struggles

What if the farm couldn’t find enough workers to harvest in time?
What if a pest wiped out half the orchard before anyone noticed?
What if rising heat and erratic rainfall made growing nearly impossible?

Now imagine that’s not just one apple or carrot — but millions, every day. Food isn’t just fuel — it’s life.

A Global Crisis

Agriculture today is unable to produce sufficient amounts of food due to labor shortages, imprecise farming, unsustainable methods, and high-tech costs.

According to the FAO, 40% of global crops are lost annually due to pests and diseases. In 2024, the U.S. agricultural industry needed 2.4 million more farm workers — and that gap is growing.

The Root of the Problem

While the world gets smarter, faster, and more digital, agriculture still depends on manual labor, guesswork, and hope.

And the ones feeding us — farmers — are struggling to survive.

Our Mission

While our focus begins with farms in the UAE, our dream extends beyond.
Project Ziraa is our commitment to building affordable agricultural automation for low-income communities across the world.

It's more than tech — it's a movement to grow smarter, together.
